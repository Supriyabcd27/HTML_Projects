Project 3: Development of AI-Based Visual Aid with Integrated Reading Assistant for the Completely Blind (at Initial research stage)

Description:

Features of the assistance system: 1. Object Recognition: Identifies and describes objects in the user's environment in real-time.
2. Scene Understanding: Provides contextual information about the surroundings, such as recognizing rooms, outdoor environments, or street crossings.
3. Text Recognition (OCR): Reads text from images or signs and converts it to speech.
4. Document Reading: Allows users to read books, documents, and labels by pointing the device at the text.
5. Currency Recognition: Identifies different currencies and their denominations.
6. Face Recognition: Identifies familiar faces and informs the user when a recognized person is nearby.
7. Navigation Assistance: Guides users through indoor and outdoor environments, avoiding obstacles.
8. Voice Interaction: Users can interact with the system via voice commands, receiving feedback through text-to-speech.

    Hardware Components:
  1. Smartphone for developing a mobile app.
  2. Bone Conduction Headphones: Allow users to hear the system’s feedback while remaining aware of their surroundings
  3. Bluetooth Microphone: For better voice recognition, especially in noisy environments.

Technologies Used: AI/ML, Android Studio, Tesseract
